30 Years of Design

Randy has been working in the design field for the past 30 years. Having obtained his BFA degree from James Madison University, he started working on print publications, moved to studio work and then to websites and interactives. He is constantly updating his skills and pays attention to current print and web design trends.

From design to coding

Randy has worked with national clients to mom and pop shops. He treats every customer with respect and works with them to create the best solution for their budget that works for their market.

From logos to exhibit design, from brochures to websites, from icons to media kits, Randy has worked on anything that you can think of. Meeting long and short term deadlines.

For 30 years he has work on print and web design and production, creating award-winning products that give the client a measureable return on their investment.
